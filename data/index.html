How to read the controls

Year, court, and topic choose which cases are included in the scenario.
Min judge cases prevents the model from moving cases to judges with very little observed data.
Capacity slack controls how much additional work a judge may receive. A value of 0.10 means up to 10% more cases than that judge handled in the selected time bucket.
Time bucket keeps assignments realistic by only moving cases among judges active in the same year, quarter, or month.
Model view switches between the main log model and a raw-days comparison. Use the log model unless you specifically want day-for-day sensitivity to very long cases.
Predicted savings means model-estimated hearing-to-judgment days saved, not guaranteed calendar time saved by the court.
